The practice

RGBI — RG Business Intelligence — was established in Ireland in 2018. The practice specialises in Power BI semantic modelling, DAX engineering, and statistical process control frameworks for organisations operating in high-stakes, regulated, or high-accountability environments.

RGBI is a sole practice. Every engagement is delivered by Ray Glass directly — not passed to a junior team after the initial scoping call. Clients engage with the person whose name is on the company, and that person delivers the work.

Why Formula 1

Formula 1 is not the fastest way to get from A to B. It is the most precisely engineered, most rigorously validated, most data-intensive form of motorsport on earth. Every decision is traceable. Every component is tested to failure before it reaches the track. Every performance gain is measured, documented, and understood.

That is the standard RGBI applies to data. Not because it is fashionable — but because organisations that rely on their data for operational decisions, board reporting, or regulatory compliance cannot afford a model that breaks when the analyst who built it leaves the room.

Most business intelligence practice is closer to a road car — it works, it gets you there, but the engineering decisions were made for cost and convenience, not precision. When it breaks, you find out why after it breaks. RGBI builds the race car: engineered to exact tolerance, validated before deployment, and documented to a standard where any qualified person can open it and understand every decision that was made.

"I do not accept standard solutions. I build semantic models that withstand scrutiny."

How RGBI is different

The market for Power BI development is saturated with generalists who can build a dashboard. RGBI does not compete in that market. The practice is positioned for engagements where the model is a governed system — where the output is used for board reporting, regulatory compliance, or operational decision-making where an incorrect number has consequences.

Clients who engage RGBI are not looking for a dashboard. They are looking for a data asset that will hold its value for years — correctly engineered, independently auditable, and capable of being extended by their own team without losing integrity.

Sector experience

RGBI has delivered governed analytics platforms across regulated infrastructure operations, B2B distribution, logistics, and commercial performance management. Common threads across all sectors: high transaction volumes, multiple stakeholders with competing definitions of "the right number," and environments where an incorrect KPI has operational or financial consequence.

That experience informs how RGBI approaches every new engagement — not with a template, but with the same rigorous design process applied to the specific grain, governance requirements, and performance questions of the client's environment.

What RGBI Values

The principles that do not move

Correctness over convenience

The correct answer, correctly computed, with documented assumptions — even when a quicker approximation would pass unnoticed. Shortcuts accumulate into technical debt that eventually breaks the model.

Auditability over agility

A model that can be built quickly but cannot be audited is not an asset — it is a liability deferred. RGBI builds for the moment when someone needs to trace a board-level number back to its source.

Client independence over dependency

Every engagement ends with the client in a stronger position — with the knowledge, documentation, and governance structures to maintain and extend the model without ongoing dependency on RGBI.
