The RGBI Standard

Not dashboards. Governed intelligence systems.

Most practitioners deliver pivot tables. RGBI delivers semantic models that hold their integrity under executive challenge, regulatory review, and organisational change.

01

Governance by Design

Every measure carries a data owner, steward, and computational lineage. Aligned to DAMA DMBOK 2.0 Platinum standards, RGBI models are built to withstand audit scrutiny — not just pass a visual review.

02

Statistical Rigour

Performance thresholds are derived from control chart theory, SPC principles, and Bayesian inference — not arbitrary red/amber/green traffic lights. RGBI treats metrics as statistical constructs, not arithmetic.

03

Architectural Precision

A governed measure hierarchy separates base metrics from board-level narrative. Filter context, evaluation order, and grain integrity are explicit by design — never left to chance or convention.
